About Carrie Lotito

Professional Life

My next endeavor was being a hostess at a resturant. following that, I was a direct support professional Connecting people with disabilities and autism to care takers. Finally, My most recent job was being a barista at Barnes and Nobles which I absolutley loved. It was such a good work environment and everyone was so kind.
